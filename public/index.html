Bio Medical Evidence Graph

A graph database for merging and analyzing connected data


storage

Explore

The BMEG is a graph database that collects data from over 20 different large scale resources and aggregates them into a single coherent resource. Sources include:

  • CCLE
  • TCGA
  • MC3
  • CTRP
  • VICC
  • Pathway Commons
  • MyVariantInfo
  • PFAM
  • GeneOntology
